Can your body portray emotions as well as your face?


Thank you for responding to this long research. There were three main objectives behind this task - To study whether the human body is able to convey emotions as well as the human face, To see if the recognition of a particular emotion is better than the recognition of other emotions, and To understand whether there is a correlation between the people who score high on an empathy scale & their ability to recognize emotions. All the pictures were shown to you via two cues - face or body and each picture portrayed either of the following four emotions ; happiness, sadness, anger and fear. It is hypothesised that there will be a difference in the recognition of emotion via each type of cue and there will also be a difference in the recognition of different emotions. Another prediction is that the people who will score high on the empathy scale will be more accurate in in recognizing emotions in comparison to people who score low on the same.
